About the Calendar

Our calendar webapp is designed to serve as a comprehensive guide for Sri Lankans to manage and understand important cultural, religious, and public events throughout the year.

This calendar includes the following key features:

  • Daily Calendar: View the current date, day of the week, and full monthly calendar interface in a user-friendly layout.
  • Sinhala and Hindu New Year Events: Learn about the traditional rituals and auspicious times (nekath) associated with the Sinhala and Hindu New Year including cleaning the house, first bath, oiling the head, and more.
  • Public & Poya Holidays: Stay updated on all Sri Lankan government holidays and full moon (Poya) days that are important for religious observances and cultural events.
  • Rahukala Times: Access daily Rahu Kaalaya periods which are traditionally considered inauspicious for starting new ventures or activities, especially in astrology and religious practices.

This digital calendar is optimized for mobile devices and ensures you never miss an important date, ritual, or national event again.
